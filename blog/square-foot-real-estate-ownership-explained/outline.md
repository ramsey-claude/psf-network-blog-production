## QuickAnswer (60 sec)
Square-foot real estate ownership expresses your stake in a property as a number of square feet rather than abstract shares. Underneath, the legal structure is the same as standard fractional platforms: an LLC holds the property; you hold a membership interest in the LLC. The difference is presentation. A 1,200 square foot property at $400 per square foot priced is the same regardless of whether you buy 50 square feet or 50 shares; the math is just easier to reason about. The model does not change risk, returns, or regulatory treatment.
