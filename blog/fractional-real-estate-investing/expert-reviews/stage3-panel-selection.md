# Stage 3 Panel Selection - fractional-real-estate-investing

## Final panel

Regulators: SEC, FINRA, CFPB
Always-on: Editorial

Total reviewers: 4 + Moderator (final)

## Justification per inclusion

- **SEC** (default + topic-triggered): Content covers Regulation A and Regulation D offerings, LLC membership interests as securities. Core SEC territory.
- **FINRA** (default + topic-triggered): Content makes investment return claims, includes risk disclosures, references broker-dealer adjacent activity (platforms acting as issuers).
- **CFPB** (default + topic-triggered): Content makes consumer-facing claims about platform fees, minimums, accessibility for non-accredited investors. Consumer protection scope.
- **Editorial** (always-on): Reader experience and brand voice are not in any regulator's scope. Required on every post.

## Justification per exclusion

- **FDIC** (default in v1, removed here): Content references FDIC only to clarify what is NOT FDIC insured. FDIC reviewer's scope is fully captured by including E10 in evidence.md and the disclaimer line. No deposit product is being marketed.
- **OCC** (excluded): No national bank lending, mortgages, or charter content.
- **Fed** (excluded): No interest rate forecasting, no monetary policy claims.
- **CFTC** (excluded): No derivatives, futures, or tokenized asset content.
- **FSOC** (excluded): No systemic risk or contagion claims.
