FLAGGED ISSUES:

1. **Hook opens with generic time anchor** - SEVERITY: MED - FIX: "A few years ago, owning rental property meant..." is fine but starts with a soft time anchor. The hook would land harder with a specific contrast in the first sentence. Suggested rewrite: "The minimum stake in a US rental property used to be a $20,000 down payment. Now it is $20." This puts the contrast (20,000 vs 20) in the first sentence, which is more share-worthy and AI-extractable.

2. **Opening second paragraph lists trade-offs without setting up the post's payoff clearly** - SEVERITY: LOW - FIX: The second paragraph names trade-offs ("control, liquidity, fees") but the "what you will get from reading this post" promise is implicit. Add one sentence: "By the end, you will know whether fractional real estate fits your situation, and how to evaluate any platform before you commit."

3. **"How does fractional real estate investing work, step by step?" is verbose for the H2** - SEVERITY: LOW - FIX: Question is fine but seven words is long. SERP titles and AI Overview cards favor punchier questions. Consider "How does fractional real estate investing work?" (six words) and move "step by step" into the answer capsule.

4. **Sources section uses "U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission" with periods three times in a row** - SEVERITY: LOW - FIX: First mention should expand it; subsequent rows can use "SEC". Minor readability fix.

5. **Related links use slug-style anchor text but the link copy is the H1 of the linked post** - SEVERITY: LOW - FIX: This is good practice (descriptive anchor) but the actual H1s of those slugs are not necessarily what I wrote. Verify each anchor matches the actual H1 in the target slug's brief or outline before publish. For now the anchor text in this draft is provisional and may need to update at publish time.
