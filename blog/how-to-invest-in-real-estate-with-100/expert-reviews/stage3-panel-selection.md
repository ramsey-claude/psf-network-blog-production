# Stage 3 Panel Selection

Final panel: SEC, FINRA, CFPB, Editorial. Moderator final.

## Inclusion

- **SEC**: Multiple platform references with specific minimums + Reg A/D mentions. Core SEC territory.
- **FINRA**: Yield ranges (4-9%), compounding tables with specific dollar figures, suitability-adjacent "$100 is enough" framing.
- **CFPB**: First-time investor ICP, "$100 is enough" framing, fee disclosures, K-1 complexity.
- **Editorial**: Hook quality, capsule compliance, brand voice, US-only check.

## Exclusions

FDIC, OCC, Fed, CFTC, FSOC: not relevant. No deposit products, no bank charters, no monetary policy, no derivatives, no systemic claims.

## Round order

1. SEC
2. FINRA
3. CFPB
4. Editorial
5. Moderator
