# Stage 3 Panel Selection

Final panel: SEC, FINRA, CFPB, Editorial. Moderator final.

## Inclusion justification

- **SEC** (default + topic-triggered): Content explains REIT structures (publicly traded, non-traded, private), fractional LLC framework, Regulation A/D references. Heavy SEC territory.
- **FINRA** (default + topic-triggered): Return comparisons between two investment structures; specific yield numbers (4% REIT, 4-10% fractional); investor suitability framing.
- **CFPB** (default + topic-triggered): Consumer-facing "which to choose" comparison; risk language consistency across both products; "safer than" comparative framing.
- **Editorial** (always-on): Hook strength, brand voice, capsule integrity, neutral comparison tone (brief explicitly flagged neutrality as key).

## Exclusions

- FDIC, OCC, Fed, CFTC, FSOC: not relevant. No deposit products, no bank lending, no monetary policy claims, no derivatives, no systemic risk.
