# Realbricks

**URL:** https://realbricks.com  
**Founded:** Platform launched July 2024  
**Headquarters:** US

---

## Model

Fractional equity ownership in individual long-term rental single-family properties. SEC Reg A+. No accreditation required. All transactions in USD (no crypto/tokenization). Properties primarily in Omaha, NE markets.

## Key Numbers

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Minimum investment | $100 (10 shares at $10/share) |
| Income frequency | Quarterly |
| Target dividend yield | 6% annual (actual Q4 2024: 9% annualized, Q1/Q2 2025: ~8% annualized) |
| Fees | Not fully disclosed publicly; outlined in offering documents |
| Liquidity | Secondary market in development (targeted H1 2026 launch) |
| Secondary market structure | Peer-to-peer, limit orders, good-till-filled orders |
| Property types | Long-term rental single-family homes |
| Current markets | Omaha, NE (primary) |

## Strengths

- Consistently outperforming own dividend projections (6% target, 8-9% actual)
- USD-only (no crypto complexity)
- Transparent quarterly dividend announcements with specific figures
- Secondary market roadmap clearly communicated
- Plaid bank integration for smooth funding
- Google partnership mentioned (details limited)

## Weaknesses

- Very new platform (July 2024 launch) - limited track record
- Quarterly distributions vs. monthly competitors
- Geographic concentration (Omaha-heavy) increases market risk
- Secondary market still in development as of early 2026
- Limited property types (LTR single-family only)
- Smaller portfolio vs. Arrived, Fundrise, Ark7

## Content Strategy

- Comparison articles vs. every major competitor (Fundrise, Arrived, Groundfloor, Yieldstreet)
- Quarterly dividend announcement posts
- Beginner educational content
- Google partnership story

## Positioning vs. psfnetwork

Realbricks is a young US-only platform building credibility through consistent dividend performance. psfnetwork operates in a different geographic lane (international/Gulf) and with a different ownership model (square-foot based).
