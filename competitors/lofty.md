# Lofty (lofty.ai)

**URL:** https://lofty.ai  
**Founded:** ~2021  
**Headquarters:** US  
**Blockchain:** Algorand

---

## Model

Blockchain-tokenized fractional ownership of US rental properties. Each property is structured as a Wyoming DAO LLC. Tokens represent ownership. Daily rental income distributed in USDC stablecoin. No accreditation required.

## Key Numbers

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Minimum investment | $50/token |
| Income frequency | Daily (in USDC stablecoin) |
| Fees | 3% trading fee + 2.9-3.9% deposit/withdrawal fees |
| Liquidity | Instant - no holding period, peer-to-peer marketplace |
| Blockchain | Algorand |
| Monthly users | ~7,000 |
| Properties | 150+ across 40 markets |
| Property management | Full-service included |
| Governance | 60% supermajority token vote for major decisions |

## Strengths

- Highest income frequency (daily) in entire category
- Instant liquidity - no holding period requirement
- Blockchain transparency (all ownership on-chain)
- No AUM fees
- Governance rights for token holders
- Full-service property management included (no extra PM fees)
- IRA-compatible via Alto IRA

## Weaknesses

- Requires crypto wallet and USDC knowledge
- No mobile app (web-only)
- 3% trading fee + high deposit/withdrawal fees eat into returns
- Regulatory uncertainty (tokenization not traditional securities)
- Only ~7,000 monthly users (small community)
- Complex tax reporting (crypto + real estate)
- Less established regulatory framework vs. SEC Reg A+
- Smaller track record than Arrived or Ark7

## Content Strategy

- Educational content on blockchain/tokenization in real estate
- Comparison articles vs. Fundrise and Ark7
- Tokenization trend and future of real estate content

## Positioning vs. PSFnetwork

Lofty targets crypto-native investors. PSFnetwork's audience is traditional investors seeking accessible international real estate - no blockchain complexity. These audiences do not significantly overlap.
