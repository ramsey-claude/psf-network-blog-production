# Yieldstreet (yieldstreetalternativeincomefund.com)

**URL:** https://yieldstreet.com  
**Note:** Rebranded as "Willow Wealth" for some products. The referenced URL (yieldstreetalternativeincomefund.com) relates to their Alternative Income Fund.  
**Founded:** ~2015  
**Regulatory structure:** SEC-registered funds

---

## Model

Multi-asset alternatives platform. Real estate is one of several asset classes alongside art, legal finance, private credit, and structured notes. Alternative Income Fund (AIF) is the main non-accredited product. Individual deal offerings typically require accreditation.

## Key Numbers

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Min. investment (Alt. Income Fund) | $10,000 |
| Income frequency | Quarterly |
| AIF total return (2025) | 3.04% |
| AIF total return since inception | 6.02% (as of Dec 31, 2025) |
| AIF fees | 1.0% annual management + up to 0.5% admin expense |
| Liquidity (AIF) | Quarterly tender offers, capped at 5% per quarter / 20% annually |
| Target investors | Accredited + non-accredited (AIF only) |
| Asset classes | Real estate, art, legal finance, private credit, structured notes |

## Strengths

- Broadest alternative asset class diversification in peer group
- Institutional-quality deal access
- Strong brand recognition in alternatives space
- Multiple asset types reduce real estate concentration risk

## Weaknesses

- High minimum ($10,000 for AIF) vs. fractional RE competitors
- Low recent returns (3.04% total return for full year 2025)
- Limited liquidity (quarterly tender offers, capped)
- 1.5% total annual fees are high
- Real estate is not the core focus
- Complex product lineup can confuse retail investors
- $10K minimum excludes many retail investors

## Content Strategy

- Alternative investment education
- Asset class comparison content
- Wealth building and portfolio diversification
- Competitor comparison articles

## Positioning vs. PSFnetwork

Yieldstreet competes for wallet share of investors who want alternatives beyond stocks. PSFnetwork is a pure-play real estate platform. Content angle: "alternatives vs. direct real estate ownership" - PSFnetwork can argue for the focus advantage.
