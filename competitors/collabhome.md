# CollabHome (collabhome.io)

**URL:** https://collabhome.io  
**Founded:** 2020 (Orinda, CA)  
**Founder:** Qian Wang (MIT, 22 years RE experience, Warburg Pincus/GIC background)  
**Regulatory structure:** SEC Reg A+

---

## Model

Fractional equity ownership exclusively in student housing properties near major US universities (UC Berkeley, UMass, Rutgers). Community-driven model where investors, tenants, and "Community Pros" can earn income by performing property management tasks. Series LLC per property.

## Key Numbers

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Minimum investment | $20/share ($500 historically cited in some sources) |
| Income frequency | Monthly |
| Target distribution yield | ~5% annual cash return |
| Target IRR (5-7 year hold) | ~15% (including appreciation) |
| Fees | 2% annual AUM + 20% of incremental value growth at sale |
| Platform co-ownership | Min. 5% of each property |
| Liquidity | No secondary market currently; early redemption program in development |
| Hold period | 3-5 years typical |
| AUM | $50M+ |
| Properties | 8+ properties |

## Strengths

- Only fractional platform focused exclusively on student housing
- Community Pro program (unique: investors/tenants earn income via property tasks)
- Monthly distributions
- Strong institutional background of founder
- Near-top universities (stable rental demand regardless of economic cycles)
- 5% platform co-ownership aligns incentives
- "Recession-proof" student housing narrative

## Weaknesses

- No secondary market (most illiquid platform in comparison)
- Niche geographic focus (near specific universities only)
- Small portfolio (8+ properties vs. hundreds at competitors)
- 2 and 20 fee model can reduce net returns at sale
- Limited to student housing (no diversification across property types)
- IPO planned for mid-2025 (status unclear as of May 2026)

## Content Strategy

- Student housing market education
- Community-driven investing narrative
- University market-specific content
- Monthly distribution announcements

## Positioning vs. psfnetwork

CollabHome is fully differentiated by niche (student housing near US universities). Zero direct competition with psfnetwork's international/Gulf real estate focus. No content overlap expected.
