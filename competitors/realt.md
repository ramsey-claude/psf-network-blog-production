# RealT (realt.co)

**URL:** https://realt.co  
**Founded:** 2019 (Boca Raton, FL)  
**Blockchain:** Ethereum + Gnosis Chain  
**Note:** As of 2025, RealT explicitly blocks US resident access. Platform serves international investors only.

---

## Model

Blockchain-tokenized fractional ownership of US residential properties. Delaware Series LLC per property, with each property split into ~1,000 ERC-20 tokens. Rent distributed in DAI or USDC stablecoins. DeFi integration allows tokens to be used as collateral.

## Key Numbers

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Minimum investment | ~$50/token |
| Income frequency | Daily/weekly stablecoin distributions |
| Gross yield range | 6-16% |
| Income distributed | $29M+ since inception (65,000+ registered investors) |
| Properties tokenized | 970+ US properties |
| Liquidity | DeFi secondary markets (Uniswap, Swapcat), peer-to-peer |
| Target investors | International (non-US residents only as of 2025) |
| Blockchain | Ethereum + Gnosis Chain |

## Strengths

- Pioneer in real estate tokenization (since 2019)
- Largest tokenized RE portfolio (970+ properties)
- Daily stablecoin income
- DeFi integration (tokens usable as collateral, staking)
- Global investor access
- On-chain transparency (all ownership verifiable)
- Sub-$10 effective minimums in some cases

## Weaknesses

- US residents blocked as of 2025 (regulatory positioning)
- Requires crypto wallet, Ethereum/Gnosis network knowledge
- Variable gas fees
- Complex tax reporting (crypto + foreign RE ownership)
- No traditional securities structure
- Regulatory uncertainty
- Third-party property management (quality varies)
- 88% of users have invested less than $5,000 (low average investment size)

## Content Strategy

- Tokenization education
- DeFi integration guides
- International real estate market content
- Blockchain transparency angle

## Positioning vs. psfnetwork

RealT is the international blockchain real estate platform. psfnetwork targets international investors but through a traditional (non-blockchain) ownership model. Potential content angle: "tokenized vs. traditional fractional real estate for international investors."
