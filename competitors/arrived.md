# Arrived

**URL:** https://arrived.com  
**Founded:** 2019 (Seattle, WA)  
**Funding:** $114M raised | Backed by Jeff Bezos (Bezos Expeditions), Forerunner Ventures, Core Innovation Capital  
**AUM:** $337M+ (as of Feb 2026)  
**Registered investors:** 945,000+  
**Occupancy rate:** 93% (Feb 2026)

---

## Model

Fractional equity ownership in individual single-family rental and vacation/short-term rental (STR) properties. Operates under SEC Regulation A+. No accreditation required.

## Key Numbers

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Minimum investment | $100 |
| Income frequency | Quarterly |
| Fees | 3.5-5% one-time sourcing + 8-25% property management + 0.15% annual AUM |
| Liquidity | Limited secondary market (quarterly trading windows, launched late 2025) |
| Early exit penalty | 2% if sold before 12 months |
| Property types | Single-family LTR + vacation/STR |
| Markets | 15+ U.S. markets, 550+ properties |

## Strengths

- Strongest brand in the category (Bezos backing drives media coverage and trust)
- Only platform offering both LTR and STR/vacation rental exposure
- Highest app rating in category (4.8/5 App Store)
- $337M AUM gives credibility with first-time investors
- 945K registered investors = largest community

## Weaknesses

- Quarterly distributions (vs. monthly on Ark7/Mogul)
- High minimum ($100) vs. Ark7 ($20)
- Limited liquidity - secondary market only launched late 2025
- Higher fee ceiling (up to 25% property management on STR assets)
- No IRA-optimized pathway compared to Ark7

## Content Strategy

- Heavy comparison content ("Arrived vs X")
- Property-specific updates and occupancy reports
- Beginner-friendly educational content ("what is fractional real estate")
- Media partnerships and PR-driven coverage

## Positioning vs. psfnetwork

Arrived targets US retail investors with brand trust from Bezos backing. psfnetwork targets investors interested in international/Gulf real estate markets - a lane Arrived does not compete in.
