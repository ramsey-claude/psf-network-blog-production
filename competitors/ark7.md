## Model

Fractional equity ownership in individual single-family and small multifamily long-term rental properties. Traditional Series LLC structure (not blockchain). No accreditation required. IRA-compatible.

## Strengths

- Lowest minimum investment for individual property selection ($20)
- Monthly income (3x more frequent than quarterly platforms)
- 0% AUM fees (vs 1% at Fundrise, 0.15% at Arrived)
- IRA-compatible with clear custodian structure
- Strongest content/SEO operation in the category
- PPEX ATS secondary market (SEC-registered ATS) - more regulated than peer-to-peer
- Platform co-invests in every property (aligns incentives)
- Simple tax reporting (1099, not K-1)
- Mobile app with strong ratings

## Weaknesses

- 12-month mandatory holding period before secondary market access
- No vacation rental / STR product (vs. Arrived)
- No blockchain/tokenization (feature for some investors)
- Geographic concentration in Sun Belt markets
- Property management fees (8-15%) reduce distributions

## Content Strategy

Ark7 runs the most aggressive blog/SEO operation in the fractional real estate space:
- Programmatic city-by-city content: "fractional real estate investing in [City]" for hundreds of US cities
- Competitor comparison articles for every major platform
- Beginner guides and investment strategy content
- FAQ and educational hub
- Effectively using blog as top-of-funnel acquisition channel
